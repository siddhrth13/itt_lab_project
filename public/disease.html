Instructions to upload

  1. Access the Upload Page:
    • Navigate to the upload page of the website.
    • This could be accessible through the main navigation menu or a specific link provided on the homepage.
  2. Select an Image:
    • Once on the upload page, you'll see a file upload section.
    • Click on the button or the designated area to choose an image file from your device.
  3. Choose an Image:
    • A file picker dialog will appear, allowing you to browse and select an image file from your computer or device.
    • Ensure that the image you choose is clear and well-lit, with the disease area clearly visible.
  4. Upload the Image:
    • After selecting the image, click on the "Upload" or "Submit" button to start the upload process.
  5. Wait for Diagnosis:
    • After the upload, the website will start processing the uploaded image to diagnose the disease.
  6. View Results:
    • Once the diagnosis is complete, you will be redirected to a results page.
    • On the results page, you'll see information about the diagnosed disease, including any relevant details or recommendations.
